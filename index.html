01

Who We Are.

SAE INDIA Founded in 1994, is an affiliate society of SAE International, registered as an Indian non-profit engineering and scientific society dedicated to the advancement of mobility community in India. As an individual member driven society of mobility practitioners, SAE INDIA comprises members who are individuals from the mobility community, which includes engineers and executives. Principal emphasis is placed on transport industries such as automotive, aerospace, and commercial vehicles.

02

What we do

SAE-AKGEC takes parts in various competitions throughout India conducted by SAE INDIA and various other organization. Students of the society work very hard for these competitions throughout the year. They are guided by their faculty advisor and other teaching staff in these competitions. The only objective of the student is to learn and win in these competions.Prime objective of SAE-AKGEC is to enhance skills of students in field automobiles and set new benchmarks in world of automobiles.

03

Our Workshops

SAE-AKGEC Conducts Various Workshops Throughout The Year To Enhance Skills Of First And Second-Year Students In Robotics, Automobiles And Various Other Fields. These Workshops Provide Students Practical Knowledge Which Enables Them To Participate In Several National And International Competition. They provide fuel to the students to become creative and learn about themselves.
